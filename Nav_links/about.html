About Us

Discover the story behind Newa Ghasa.

Our History

Newa Ghasa has been serving authentic Nepali cuisine for over a decade. Founded by passionate food lovers, our restaurant brings together traditional recipes and modern techniques to offer an unforgettable dining experience.

Our journey began with a small food stall in the heart of Kathmandu. Over the years, we expanded to a full-fledged restaurant, gaining a loyal following and recognition for our dedication to preserving the rich culinary heritage of Nepal. Our team continues to strive for excellence, bringing you dishes that are both authentic and innovative.

Our Mission

At Newa Ghasa, our mission is to provide a dining experience that celebrates the vibrant flavors and traditions of Nepali cuisine. We are committed to using high-quality ingredients and traditional cooking methods to create dishes that honor the cultural heritage of Nepal.

We believe in fostering a warm and welcoming environment where our guests can enjoy authentic Newari dishes, share stories, and create lasting memories. Our dedication to excellence in both food and service is at the heart of everything we do.
